Frequently Asked Questions

Why are GitLab pipelines slow?

Inefficient jobs, lack of caching, or poor runner configuration.

What is GitLab CI?

A continuous integration system built into GitLab.

How can I optimize pipelines?

Use caching, parallel jobs, and efficient runners.

What is a runner?

An agent that executes CI/CD jobs.

How do I debug failures?

Check logs, isolate steps, and validate configurations.
